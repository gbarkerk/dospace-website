What is the DoSpace coworking space?

A fun and quirky working space located on a barge. At 800SqF and with only 8 work stations, the main office is bright and spacious. We also have a work bar - hot desk area - for those who like to drop in and out. Members get access to the barge, become part of a close community and can take advantage of great deals on corporate health plans, GoCar and AirBnB.

Where are you located?

We are located on Grand Canal Dock, Dublin 2 - Silicon Docks. We are meters from the Google complex, Facebook, LinkedIn and AirBnB not to mention tens of other tech companies in the area. This is the area to be in Dublin. Within a three minute walk we have the DART, three Dublin bike scheme stations and a GoCar station. DoSpace members can avail of special GoCar rates.

Being on a boat sounds cool but how does it work as a coworking space?

Our barge was once a boat hotel and built as such so there is plenty of natural light, 220V mains power and central heating. There is also a fully functional bathroom and kitchen. In fact we are so office like we even have a water cooler for those all important chats. The only time you will notice you are on a boat is stepping on and off the vessel, looking out the window at the great view, or taking those summer meetings on deck. Note: Being a boat there are limitations around disabled access.

Is the boat secure, can I leave my laptop or a monitor there?

The boat is very secure. We are fully alarmed. The boat is also securely locked (soon to be accessed via the DoSpace app). Entry to the dock is via two accessed controlled gates. There is CCTV. On board, 'homemaker' members can request lockable filing cabinets under their desk.

How does membership work and can I subscribe month to month?

Membership is monthly with no fixed contract. We operate on a move in first day of the month, move out last day of the month policy. If you decide you would like to move on from DoSpace just let us know through your DoSpace app or talk with your community manager before the first of the month that you wish to leave.

What is included in membership?

  • Furniture; table, chair, locker, desk lamp, whiteboard
  • Internet; 42 Mbps, WI-FI as standard, Ethernet by request
  • Conference room time
  • Prints/Copies/Scannings
  • Access to the DoSpace Network and fun events 
  • Refreshments; tea, coffee, soft-drinks 
  • Extras; XBox in the box, christmas party
  • Reduced healthcare, mobile phone packages carrental

Can you facilitate meetings both adhoc internal and with clients?

Yes we can, DoSpace is all about collaboration and meetings both planned and ad-hoc are essential for this. We have a small five person meeting room on board with a large white board for those inter-team brainstorming sessions. For presentations and client meetings we have access to the unique meeting room in the iconic Irish Waterways building located right beside our barge.

Currently I am a one person company but I hope to grow in the coming months. Can I expand my company with DoSpace?

This is a exactly how we work, you can scale up or down as required. Just inform us of your needs before the first of the month and we will make sure to accommodate you. We are currently designing our first stand alone office so there are plenty of opportunities.

Can I see the facility before I decide?

Of course, we would love to meet you and give you a tour. If you like what you see, have a half days use of the space on us. Just call us at 01 539 7935 or chat to us on our website to discuss setting up a tour.

What is the address add-on?

For those that would like a Dublin 2 mailing address, we will operate a basic secretary service and collect and deliver your mail. Each morning it will be on your desk or forwarded to an address of your choosing. This comes free for 'homemaker' members and as an extra add-on for casual members.

Can you provide my company with a phone service?

Yes we provide VOIP services as a monthly add-on. If you already have a number that you require to move with you this can also be facilitated. On board we have a phone booth for taking those private calls.

How much deposit is required and when?

We request one months deposit payable at time of booking and a €25 setup fee per desk when on the 'Homemaker' membership. Payment is accepted via credit card, paypal or wire transfer.

How do I pay my membership?

Membership must be paid before the first of the month. Payment is accepted by direct debit (preferred). One off payments by credit/debit card is acceptable.

Do I need to take out insurance?

The barge has public liability insurance. Contents are at your own risk. We are happy to provide content insurance if you will be leaving items of value on board.

What hours is DoSpace open?

There will be staff onsite from 9:00am till 6:00pm, Monday - Friday. You will receive your own access card/Dospace app which will enable access to the facility at any time you wish.

Can clients/visitors/staff from other offices visit?

Certainly, we are a working community so the more people that visit the better. There is a method to check in visitors via the Dospace app. If work colleagues intend to visit for the day, we would ask that you purchase them a day drop-in pass. They can then work from their own hot desk if they wish. This can be confirmed with your community manager.

Can I refer a friend to DoSpace?

Of course, we know that people like to work with others they know. Any referral will receive 10% off their first months membership (to say thanks for making the community better, the referrer does too!).
