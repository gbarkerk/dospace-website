The Barge

That is right! Our co-working community is based on a river barge!

The "Grey Owl"

One of the premier barges of the Irish Waterways the "Grey Owl" is a dry open plan space; ideal for community interaction or quiet solo work.

800 Square feet of centrally heated workspace, the barge is always at the perfect temperature. Internet and power points available throughout. It even has a beer tap!


History

Following recent conversion, the "Grey Owl" moved to Dublin and is now located on the Grand Canal Dock Marina.

The Grey Owl was custom Built in 2008 by Bluewater boats, UK as a luxury boatel, prior to conversion to Ireland's first floating co-working space.

Workstations

Individual standard work-stations are 1,200mm x 600mm in size, and include two power-points, desk light and executive chair. Each work space is located next to a window with an amazeing view of the Dock, giving a bright and airy feel. Options include LAN connections, storage units, LED monitors, Cisco IP handsets, whiteboard, larger desks.

zoom

The Cube Conference Room

Metres from the barge is the iconic Irish Waterways Cube Building containing our formal meeting and conference room. Guaranteed to impress your customers and investors with seating for up to 12. Equipped with HD projector, screen, Chromecast connectivity and surround sound. It can be booked via the DoSpace application. Additional catering can be supplied.

zoom

The Wheelhouse Conference Room

Our informal meeting room, cleverly called "The Wheelhouse" as it is the boat's actual wheelhouse is located on the upper deck. With seating for up to eight it is bookable via the DoSpace application. Although equipped with a 37inch HD screen, Chromecast connectivity, surround sound, it's most noticeable feature is a full 360 degree view of Grand Canal Dock.

Other Perks

On board we have a canteen with free coffee, tea and soft drinks, a seating/breakout area, a private phone booth and whiteboards. With direct access to legal, accounting and marketing experts we can link you in to whomever you need. Printing and copying are included in each membership.

The DoSpace Community

Our community is the hub of our co-working space. Lunch, chat, attend networking events and share knowledge with your fellow members. "The Christmas Run", one of our networking program highlights invites all DoSpace members to host their Christmas parties on board up the river!

ManageMySpace™ App

ManageMySpace™ App is an app designed and developed by DoSpace to make every members life easy. Via the ManageMySpace™ App you can book conference rooms, extend/shorten your contract, communicate with everybody else in the DoSpace community and even use it to gain keyless access to DoSpace spaces.
